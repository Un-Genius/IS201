About

George C. Ramsay is a college student at BYU majoring in Information Systems (BS) with a minor in Business Management. George has experience in entrepreneurship, programming, drone operations, and marketing. His volunteer experience with The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints has given him diverse skills spanning public speaking, communication, and leadership.

Having previously been involved in coordinating game development, George is now concentrating on furthering his expertise in project management. He plans to graduate BYU and aims to entrepreneur in AI consulting, helping integrate modern solutions into daily life. To work to that goal George is looking for any work related to Business and CS to develop real-world experience.
